With over 25 years of experience

Our 10,000 sq ft facility in Eden Prairie, MN houses all operations from engineering and sales to production. Rackmaster designs and manufactures a full line of rackmount computer chassis, peripheral enclosures and computer systems. Rackmaster provides diversified and market niche solutions to a wide array of industrial, defense, broadcasting, solution provider, telecommunications, command and control, and embedded applications.
